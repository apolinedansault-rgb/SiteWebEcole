The birth of a collaboration

When two icons meet. It all started with a shared obsession: the human experience in effort. Nike has always believed that sport is far more than physical performance. It is a mindset, an emotion, a moment suspended between who you are and who you can become. Spotify, on its side, has transformed music into daily fuel — the right sound, at the right moment, changes everything.

In 2024, the innovation teams of both companies came together around a simple but radical question: what if music no longer followed the runner, but ran with them? From that conversation was born Nike Flow — the world's first running shoe that automatically synchronises your playlist to your running pace, in real time, via the Spotify algorithm.

Why this collaboration is powerful

Two leaders, one shared user. The numbers confirm it: 96% of athletes listen to music during exercise. Among them, Spotify dominates with over 52% market share. Nike and Spotify did not simply partner up — they realised they were already serving the same person, at the same moment. All that was missing was a bridge between the two.

Nearly 6 out of 10 runners interrupt their workout to change a song. That simple gesture breaks the flow, disrupts the rhythm, shatters concentration. Nike Flow eliminates this problem at its root. The shoe detects your cadence, your speed, your intensity — and Spotify adapts the music accordingly. Automatically. Silently. Perfectly.

The vision

We believe every stride deserves its own soundtrack. That music is not an accessory to effort — it is its engine. That technology must fade behind the experience, not replace it. With Nike Flow, we are not selling you just another connected shoe. We are offering you something no one has ever been able to give you before: a run that is entirely yours, from the first step to the last breath.
